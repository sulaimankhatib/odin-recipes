Strawberry Banana Blended Baked Oats

Description

These strawberry banana blended baked oats are easy to make, kid friendly, and packed with healthy ingredients. Everything comes together in a blender and bakes up into a perfectly portioned, delicious treat that tastes just like having cake for breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 overripe banana
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk or milk of choice
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sweetener of choice
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh strawberries
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Combine oats, banana, eggs, almond milk, honey, baking powder, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt in a high speed blender and blend until smooth. Remove the blade and fold in chopped strawberries
  3. Divide batter evenly between 4 ramekins and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until baked oats are puffed and light golden brown, about 25 minutes.
  5. Let cool for at least 5 minutes and serve warm or at room temperature.
