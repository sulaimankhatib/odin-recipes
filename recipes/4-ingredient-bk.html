Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Cinnamon Sugar
  • Country Crock Original Spread

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Spread Country Crock on each slice of bread.
  3. Mix the sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl, then sprinkle it over the bread.
  4. Bake the cinnamon toast for 10 minutes.
  5. Turn on the broiler and broil until the sugar is bubbling, but not burned
